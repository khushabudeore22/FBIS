Indian cricketer

Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is a celebrated Indian cricketer, nicknamed "Hitman," known for his explosive batting, especially in limited-overs formats, holding records for most ODI double centuries and most T20I centuries, and for leading India to the 2024 T20 World Cup title.

Shubman Gill

Shubman Gill is a prolific young Indian cricketer, a right-handed top-order batsman known for his elegance and scoring ability across Test, ODI, and T20 formats, captaining India and the IPL's Gujarat Titans

Virat Kohali

Virat Kohli (born Nov 5, 1988) is an Indian cricket superstar, a record-breaking right-handed top-order batsman, former Indian captain across all formats, known as the "Chase Master
